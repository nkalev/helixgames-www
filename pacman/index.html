How to Play: Navigate the maze and eat all the pellets while avoiding the four ghosts. The game starts when you press any arrow key!

Power Pellets: Eat the large cyan pellets to turn the ghosts blue for a limited time. While blue, you can eat them for bonus points (200 pts each)!

Strategy: Plan your route to collect all pellets efficiently. Use power pellets wisely to clear ghosts from your path. Watch out for the tunnel on the sides - you can escape through it!

Ghosts: Each ghost has its own personality - some chase you directly, others try to cut you off. Stay alert and keep moving!
